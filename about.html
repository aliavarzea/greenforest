Nothing adds more beauty and comfort to our homes and offices than the lush flowers and foliage of indoor plants. Bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchens, cubicles...
There really isn't a space a houseplant can't enliven. Just add light and water, and you've got a growing indoor oasis.
Bringing plants into your home is aesthetically pleasing and - amazingly - plants can offer strong health benefits as well!
This is why here at Green forest we give you ideas and tips on how to care for your own plants.
